Hey, I'm

Dylan Astrup

and I'm running to be your SGA Vice-President!

How can I help you?

  • Communicating with Students
    I want all students to be informed of all council activities. In order to do this, I would advocate for a variety of new communication methods to be added to SGA, such as a podcast that could include administration and student interviews and campus updates, video posts on our Instagram, as well as more student interaction at our monthly Townhalls.
  • Administration Involvement
    I often hear that students feel disconnected from campus administration, and inviting them to attend events around campus and just being around students more would create a better environment and connection between the students and administration.
  • Council Legacy
    In this role, I would seek to continue and build upon the projects, initiatives, and general progress that this past SGA council has achieved. Another major aspect of the Vice-President's role is keeping the council members accountable for their job role and projects. I would be more than excited to help support my fellow council members on their projects and campaign goals in order to better serve the student body.
