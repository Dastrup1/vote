Why should you vote for me?

The Treasurer is one of the most important positions on the SGA council. They determine where YOUR money is spent, so you need someone that knows what they're doing. I've been an executive board member on the SGA Student Treasury for the past year, I've attended all the Budget Committee meetings this school year, and I've had past student council experience as a Treasurer. To summarize - I know a thing or two, and I want to use that knowledge to help better the student body!

How can I help you?

Here's three main initiatives that I plan to focus on as Treasurer

  • TRANSPARENCY - I want to bring the entire student body into making the SGA financial decisions, after all, it is your money. I want to be clear and transparent with the student body about where your money is being spent, and listen to YOU if you think it can be better spent other ways.
  • STUDENTS VOICE - I want to be approachable and welcome students to make suggestions and be a part of the SGA decision making. The SGA is built to represent students, and the only way to do that is to listen to the students.
  • SGA SERVICES - As a member of the Student Treasury, I have learned a lot about potential services that we could implement on campus to benefit the student body. Some of those services include adding recreation equipment rentals, fitness classes, increased club funding, and potentially a large spring event similar to OctoberWest. As treasurer, I hope to bring those services to life as well as expand upon them to create a continually evolving SGA.
